We used to be clueless!

Its almost impossible these days, to get an internship if you’re in year 1 or 2. (trust us, we’ve been there).
We strongly believe that what matters more is your personality, your skills and your ability to get shit done.
(Year 3s and 4s can apply too, we care more about your capability than your seniority.)



Our Vision And Mission

We want to make applications fair, simple and less painstaking.
What do you have to do now?

Step 1

Register yourself on Clueless

Step 2

Select the companies you want to apply to

Step 3

Fill in the necessary details, upload the necessary documents, and put your heart into answering the questions the companies want answered.

Step 4

Voila! You’re done. We’ll get in touch with you if you get through.

Don’t do what everyone else does because it’s safe, convenient or because it pays.
Figure out what you were really put on this Earth to do
