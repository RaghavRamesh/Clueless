It’s got to be easier than that, and since we don’t think you have enough help, we’re here!

Our Vision And Mission

We want to make applications fair, simple and less painstaking.
What do you have to do now?

Step 1

Register yourself on Clueless

Step 2

Give us details on the roles you can offer these students (we mainly try to help the Freshmen and Sophomores, but senior students apply as well)

Step 3

Ask the necessary questions, provide the tasks you want fulfilled (it can range from uploading a video answering a question of your choice, to doing a verbal test)

Step 4

Voila! You’re done. We’ll get in touch with you if you with interested applicants
